time station

Time, must be more efficient with it. There is limited time in a day, which limits your weeks, months, years, goals, ambition, happiness. Spend time not on what asks for it now or in another minute, but on what aligns with your passion and intentioned purpose.

The man at the Costco gas station chose to take his time backnig into the front spot to pump gas. He was too far so he decided to adjust his car and bring it closer, slowly. He decided to answer the machine's prompts, slowly, using the wrong card at times. He still pumped his car with gas but decided to not be hasty. That was his choice.

You need to push yourself. Concede or complete?
